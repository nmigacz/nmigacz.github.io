Overview

In this role, Nick was the Web Designer & Developer on the Digital Communication team for Graduate & Professional Admission (GPA) at Loyola University Chicago. Nick and his team focused on three main goals to refresh the digital presence and the admission process for GPA. The focuses included single sourcing information to have the GPA website contain the most up-to-date information, refreshing and recentering the GPA digital presence and developing a new centralized recruitment website.
