In June 2023, Nick started at his current postion as a Web Design Specialist for Ann & Robert H. Lurie Children's Hospital of Chicago Foundation where he is focused on curating the digital donation process. When Nick is not at his desk, he can be found quilting, rollerblading along the lakefront or reading a book!
