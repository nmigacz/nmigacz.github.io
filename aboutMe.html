about

In May 2021, Nick graduated from Loyola University Chicago where he majored in Software Engineering with a Visual Communication minor. For the past year, Nick has been working on developing his front end development and UI/UX skills and even had the opportunity to be commissioned by the Theater Department to build a website for an online play. Make sure to check out the projects section to see that work! Nick is currently a User Experience Intern with UL (Underwriters Laboratories) for the 2021 summer. When Nick is not programming or prototyping, he can be found sketching, going for a run or reading a book!
