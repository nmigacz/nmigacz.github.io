about

In May 2021, Nick graduated from Loyola University Chicago where he majored in Software Engineering with a Visual Communication minor. During that summer, Nick was a User Experience Intern with UL (Underwriters Laboratories) which proved to be a valuable experience. In August 2021, Nick was hired as the Web Designer for Graduate and Professional Admission at Loyola University Chicago where he continues to work presently. When Nick is not programming or designing, he can be found sketching, walking along the lakefront or reading a book!
