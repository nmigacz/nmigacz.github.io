50 Stolen Plays

Website for the digital performance of 50 Stolen Plays, a Loyola University Chicago Theater production.

Overview

In early September 2020 a professor from the Loyola Theater Department reached out to me and asked if I would build a website for one of the plays they were going to hold online. The play is called 50 Stolen Plays and consists of a combination of 50 videos, photos and PowerPoints. Understanding the director's vision, I designed the website which is in a choose your own adventure style. The theme follows that of the content, layers of imitation, which can be seen in the choice to style it after a 90's tv with incorporations such as the bouncing DVD and the use of a remote to navigate the pages.
