Team Up

An app built to help CSGO players connect

Overview

Working in React Native, my group and I built an application which serves the purpose to connect CSGO players around the world quickly, called Team Up. The app connects players based on similar ranks and regions. This eliminates time wasted on searching for a teammate as just getting into the queue will generate currently available teammates for the user. My role in the project consisted of working on the login and sign up pages. The user can choose either path from the landing page and we decided to make the user enter their STEAM username as the one for our app so to make it easier for the user to quickly add players in CSGO. In addition to the two pages, I designed the Team Up Logo seen on the landing, login and sign up pages.
